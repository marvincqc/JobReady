Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: January 1, 2025 🔄 Last updated: March 2026
JobReady is a web-based resume builder that helps foreign workers create resumes for job applications in Singapore. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1 Who We Are

JobReady is operated as a resume-building service connecting foreign workers with manpower agencies in Singapore. By using JobReady, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

When you use JobReady, we collect the information you provide during the form flow, which may include:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Nationality and current location (country and city)
  • Phone number and email address
  • Work experience, job titles, and company names
  • Skills, certifications, and education level
  • Preferred job type, work arrangement, and expected salary
  • Languages spoken
  • A generated submission ID used to track your resume request
  • The name of the manpower agency your resume is submitted to

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To generate your resume as a PDF document
  • To submit your resume to the manpower agency you specify
  • To store your resume securely so it can be forwarded to the relevant agency

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond resume submission.

4 How We Store Your Information

Your resume data and generated PDF are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service. Access is restricted to authorised personnel only. Your data is not stored on third-party messaging platforms through this website.

5 Who We Share Your Information With

Your information is shared only with:

  • The manpower agency you specify at the start of the conversation — your resume is submitted to them for employment matching purposes
  • JobReady's operator — who reviews submissions and forwards resumes to agencies

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties for commercial purposes.

6 Data Retention

Your resume and personal information are retained for as long as necessary to facilitate your job application. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9 below).

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the service

8 Third-Party Services

JobReady uses Supabase to store resume data and generated files. If we link to other services in the future, those services may have their own privacy policies. We only access the information you submit through the JobReady website.
